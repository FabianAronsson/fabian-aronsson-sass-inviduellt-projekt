Super Mario 64

Super Mario 64 is a 3D platformer in which the player controls Mario through several courses. The player gathers stars in each course; some stars only appear after completing certain tasks, often hinted at by the name of the course.

Super Mario Sunshine

Mario and Peach go on a vacation to Isle Delfino. But their trip soon gets spoiled after an imposter disguised as Mario paints and pollutes the entire island. Mario's vacation goes horribly wrong when he finds someone has polluted Isle Delfino, his destination.

Super Mario Galaxy

Bowser kidnaps Princess Peach and takes her to the center of the universe where he is creating his own galaxy to control the whole universe. It is up to Mario, Luigi and new friends to travel galaxy after galaxy until they find her.
